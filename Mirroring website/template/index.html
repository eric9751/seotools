About US

Henan CamelWay Machineray specializes in the design, manufacture and marketing of our superior Concrete Batching Plants, Concrete Mixers, Batching Machine, Concrete Pumps, Screw Conveyors, Cement & Fly Ash Silos,Concrete Trucks, Stabilized Soil Mixing Plants, and numerous other top quality machines. Based in Henan, the midland of China, we are concrete and construction machinery manufacturers and exporters. As manufacturers we offer quality machinery and equipments for road & civil construction applications. We understand that our job is much more than being construction equipment manufacturers, we are responsible to all the people who are concerned with this road and building construction industry and we strive to provide the best quality, price and services.

There are more than 1000 CamelWay concrete plants running all over the world in different climatic conditions helping to build better infrastructure, better cities and better life. We are aware of how important is the role of a concrete plant in completing a project. Keeping it in mind we design stable and long lasting structures, we have one of the best after-sales teams which is ready to help in minutes where possible and we use high-quality components which are available worldwide to make sure that our customers rely on equipment they purchased and are not bind to us in solving minor service issues.
